Whether it is playing video games, surfing the internet, or compiling code, my passion has always been for computers and technology. That passion has ultimately led me to decide on my dream career, software engineering. To achieve this goal, I plan on first taking all the engineering courses offered by my high school that will allow me to develop my knowledge about computer science and engineering as a whole. Then, I hope to attend a well-known engineering college such as University of California, Berkeley, and graduate with a masters degree in computer science to build a path towards becoming a successful software engineer. Software engineering brings out the talents and abilities within me and puts them to good use, while allowing me to to express myself in a unique way.

I have developed these traits in the experiences I have faced over the years. I enjoy creating works of art and playing piano as it brings forth my creativity and musical ability. The major accomplishments that I have achieved throughout my life also reflect these traits. My most memorable accomplishment was passing the Certificate of Merit Level 10 piano test. It required daily practice and continuous persistence over the course of ten years to finally achieve this goal.

I was able to collaborate very well with my team members solved many problems such as using time efficiently and acquiring tools to speed up the process. I learned from this experience that I was a very open-mined person as I was eager to accept the ideas of others instead sticking to my own ideas. I also have a strong set of working skills including proficiency in the HTML, CSS, Python, and Java programming languages which will aid me in becoming a successful software engineer.
